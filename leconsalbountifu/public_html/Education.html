Education


Formal Education

We are running educational Institutions for Economically weaker section at Government Fee Structure only for providing higher learning skills among the students for a better society.

Education in our country plays a very important role in the overall development. It not only helps in the development of the human resources, but also in improving the quality of life at home and society. Educated women not only tend to promote education of their girl children, but also can provide better guidance, and moreover educated women can also help in the reduction of infant mortality rate and growth of the population.
