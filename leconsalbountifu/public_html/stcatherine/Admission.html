Admission

Eligibility D.T.Ed.

AGE:

(1). The candidate should not exceed the age of 30 years.

(2).For SC/ST and physically handicapped maximum age limit 35 years.

(3).For widows, Destitute and orphans the maximum age limit is 40 years.

(4).The Age will be calculated 31st July of that year.

Academic Qualification

(1). The candidate should pass +2 standard of any medium but the candidate should Study at least Tamil subject with the minimum of 45% marks.

(2).For SC/ST students +2 pass is sufficient.
