CURRICULUM

Activites::-

Our Instituion has a reputed name in teaching the subjects and other moral classes.We proudly announce that our Teacher Training Institute was awarded for the best "Teacher Training Institute Award" by EVM Seva Paalam.This award was recevied by our Chairman from the Minister of TamilNadu state.

The students are taught with good skills in a neat enviornment and guided with talented Staff. The instruction given by the Board of Examination are strictly followed as per the time-table schedule where all the subjects are taught by our talented and highly qualified Staff.The subjects are guided well and the Tests and the model exams are conducted at regular intervals.

During the teaching methods the students are trained in using be modern tools like LCD Projector, Computers, etc.,. The use of LCD projector has made our students to gain ample skill in presenting the seminars and other subject oriented topics.

Seminar:-

Seminars are conducted more frequently for the students to develop their learning skills. By doing this the students get awareness in using the LCD projectors and Computers. The seminars are conducted either individually or in groups based on the topic given. The students are guided by the Staff in presenting the seminar.

Moral Education

Other than the normal activities, we conduct the moral education classes based on student’s discipline, helping nature, interactions, faith in God and cleanliness. The staff conducts Social and Cultural activities based on the personal skill of the students.

Computer Education

The subject classes are also conducted through computers to bring awareness in using the modern technology like computer where the students gain the subject knowledge much more interestingly through computers, especially the language subjects. By this the students develop their subjects contents very easily through this technology.Language Lab is made use of the subject like English for developing the Spoken English classes and to face interviews by the students.

• We provide priority to the Christians and other Dalits.

Our institution provides the specific techniques, that enhance the student -teacher trainees to learn about the significance in learning ability in the classroom.They are like:

1. Teaching the importance of using teaching aids.

2. Active based learning (ABL) method.

3. Teaching effective methods of Active Learning Method (ALM )and ABL methods.

4. The Trainees should teach with the help of ABL Method from Ist Standard to IVth Standard and teach ALM method for Vth Standard students.

5. We make highly efficient teacher trainees for the future generation.

6. Computer based and new innovative techiniques are taught to the students.

1.Course started:-

As per NCTE norms Teacher training course started on July _____ in our Institution with all facilities.

2.Teaching practice:-

Teacher training first year and second year students are completed fifty days (40+10) teaching practice from the particular schools. Lecturers are visited the students when their teaching practice and also they submitted all the reports. The T.P forms are given by the Directrates at the time of teaching practice. Three T.P forms are filled by the students, school guide teacher and the lecturer also, Lecturer and students discussed about their doubts in between the teaching practice periods.

3.Commission:-

D.T.Ed Commissions held on may__. The students are submitted all records, Assignments and Album works and also submitted working and non-working models. The externals (Examiner) are supervised and ask few questions about their models and teaching methods.

The students are compulsory taken the seminar classes at the time of Commission. The external alloted the marks depends on the each students performance.

4.Tests:-

Tests for the each units of all subjects compulsory write down by the Lecturers. After the unit tests the students are prepared for the revision tests, and model exams. Finally the students are ready to the examination.
