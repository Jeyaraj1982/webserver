Le Consal Bountiful Trust (LCBT) was registered as a charitable Trust under the Indian Trust Act of 1882. It is a non Governmental organization and it is exempted from Income Tax. It is empowered to receive Foreign Contribution under Foreign Contribution Regulation Act of 1976.

We're committed to making the world a better place to live by using simple online ways to address the health and well-being of people, education, and educational research, medical relief to the sick social and economical relief to the needy and to bring awareness on environment issues. Our organization is a non-profitable organization working to eradicate poverty and hunger, promote peace, address cancer and other widespread health problems, foster literacy and provide education, preserve vital habitats in peril, and to provide support to all initiatives that control and arrest global warming.
